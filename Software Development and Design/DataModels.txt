- structures for computer

- open and extensible
- self describing
- long lifespan


XML:
+ legacy support
+ supported by SOAP
- long

JSON:
+ popular
+ lightweight
+ native to JS
+ compressed to a single line, whitespace does not have any meaning
- short

YAML:
+ human friendly
+ compact
+ ideal for configuration
- whitespace matters
